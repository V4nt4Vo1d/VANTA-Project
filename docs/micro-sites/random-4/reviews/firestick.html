Fire Stick

★☆☆☆☆

I bought this because I assumed, reasonably, that it would be a stick to light fires. Like a torch. Like something to be used on a long camping trip to start the bonfires.

Instead it’s just a plastic rectangle that connects to the TV. No flames, no sparks, not even warm to the touch.

I tried rubbing two Fire Sticks together hoping to generate at least a little heat. Nothing. My ancestors are ashamed.
