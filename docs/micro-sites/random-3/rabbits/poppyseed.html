About Poppyseed

Poppyseed is a calm little rabbit who prefers to sit and observe like she’s quietly evaluating the room. She gives slow blinks, not because she’s mystical or dramatic, but because she genuinely can’t be bothered to rush through anything. She’s gentle, sweet, and slightly judgmental in a very endearing way.
