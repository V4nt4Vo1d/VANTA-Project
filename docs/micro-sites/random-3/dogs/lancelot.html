Lancelot

Golden Retriever · Overly Polite Knight

About Lancelot

Lancelot is the only dog we’ve ever met who apologizes to his toys before chewing on them. He gently boops each one like he’s knighting it into service. If you drop something on the floor, he’ll retrieve it with the solemn dignity of a medieval squire completing an oath-bound quest. He is 80% tail wag, 20% honor code.
