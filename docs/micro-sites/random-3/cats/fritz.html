Fritz

Maine Coon · Amateur birdwatcher

About Fritz

Fritz carries himself like he ruled a woodland kingdom in a past life. With a mane that suggests “final evolution stage,” Fritz lounges regally, blinks judgmentally, and maintains a calm wisdom that may or may not be fake. He enjoys birdwatching, sunbeams, and pretending he didn’t just fall off the couch.
