Nimbus

Very fluffy white angora rabbit

About Nimbus

Nimbus is basically a gentle puff of fluff with legs. She loves disappearing into blankets, hiding behind pillows, and pretending you can’t see her even when she’s extremely visible. She’s shy but curious, and once she trusts you, she melts into your arms like a warm, fuzzy marshmallow.
