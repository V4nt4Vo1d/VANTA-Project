Our Mission

Founded accidentally after someone said “wouldn’t it be funny if…,” our rescue now proudly supports a wide variety of nonexistent animals. From cats made of stars to dogs who smell like pastries, we’re committed to giving every fictional creature a permanent place on the internet.

What We Actually Do

This site is just for fun — a place to laugh, enjoy cute animals, and maybe share a page with a friend. If you're inspired to adopt, foster, or help real animals, please contact your local shelters.
