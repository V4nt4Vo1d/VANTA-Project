About Brioche

Brioche smells faintly — but undeniably — like freshly baked bread. No one knows why. We’ve checked. There is no scientific explanation. He is warm, round, soft, and has the energy of a dog who would politely ask if you'd like butter with your emotional support. Brioche waddles, snorts, naps, and radiates comfort like a living carb.
