About Tinker

Tinker has the posture of someone who definitely knows where the missing squeaky toys went — but legally cannot disclose that information. He sits like a tiny crime boss evaluating your usefulness to his organization. If a toy disappears in this household, Tinker “wasn’t even there,” but also “has an associate who can look into it.”
